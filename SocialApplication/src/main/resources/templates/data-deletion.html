Data Deletion Instructions

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from our system, please follow these steps:

For Logged-in Users:
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to your account settings
  3. Click on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your choice
If You Can't Access Your Account:

Please send an email to privacy@yourapp.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with the account
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • Your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for legal or operational purposes
